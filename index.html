About Unfetter

Unfetter is a neurofeedback training system that uses real time EEG. Unfetter helps individuals train high concentration for peak performance, emotional regulation, and a greater sense of well-being. Unfetter uses Z-Score training, based off of cutting-edge neuroscience research
